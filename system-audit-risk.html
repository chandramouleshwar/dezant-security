System Audit & Risks

Do you need to know how your estate fits together and its’ dependencies, network connections and support status? Are your IT systems maximizing your business potential and do they meet your business needs?



Our Review and Audit service allows you to review, assure and audit aspects of your Information technology Services, infrastructure and commercial contracts. To ensure that your systems are working at an optimum level and that there are no compliance issues, Dezant Consulting can undertake an extensive review of your infrastructure stack, application landscape, software and licensing, websites, intranets and portals.


The IT audit reports we produce will help you identify any areas of duplication, inefficiency or wastage. It will also help you identify any areas of concern, thus enabling you to take remedial actions, in order to ensure total peace of mind and enable you to focus on your core business. We can also undertake due diligence and a complete valuation of your systems & infrastructure assets
