Introduction

Employees are a company’s greatest asset, but also its greatest security risk. “If we look at security breaches over the last five to seven years, it’s pretty clear that employees represent the single most important point of failure in terms of security vulnerabilities,” said Eddie Schwartz, chair of ISACA’s Cyber Security Advisory Council.


In the past, organisations could train employees once a year on best practices for security – This is no longer the case. According to (ISC)2, you need to consistently update employees with the latest security vulnerabilities and train them on how to recognize and avoid them.


Education is prevention – This is why Dezant Consulting, in conjunction with our Nigeria & UK partners now offers a series of technical training with a view to capacity build for all staffs and employees of corporate Ghana and across the ECOWAS region. Our trainers & speakers are highly skilled InfoSec professionals from the United Kingdom, and we provide knowledge and education to your technical of all organisations in the private and public sectors.

Certified Information Systems Auditors (CISA)

The Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) course will give your employees the requisite skillsets to govern and control enterprise IT and perform an effective security audit on your organisation. Further, the CISA training will enhance their understanding of IS audit processes and also help them understand how to protect information systems . CISA is one of the hottest areas in information security, with a need for six million IT security professionals by 2019.


Who Should Attend?


Members of staff in I.T, Audit and Risk Managers or Information Security Management.


Duration


4 Days

Certified Information Security Managers (CISM)

The CISM certification training will give you the requisite skillsets to design, deploy and manage security architecture for your organization. The course is aligned with ISACA best practices and is designed to help you pass the CISM exam on your first attempt. In addition, this course will see that you are well-equipped to manage the ongoing security, compliance and governance of your IT organization.


Who Should Attend?


Members of staff in Information Security Management, compliance & governance.


Duration


5 Days

CISSP Certification Course Overview

The CISSP certification training develops your expertise in defining the IT architecture and in designing, building, and maintaining a secure business environment using globally approved information security standards. The course covers industry best practices and prepares you for the CISSP certification exam held by (ISC)².


CISSP Training Key Features


  • 67 hours of in-depth learning
  • 5 simulation test papers to prepare you for CISSP certification
  • Offers the requisite 30 CPEs for taking the CISSP examination
