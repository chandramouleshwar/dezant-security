Sector Training

Cyber Security For Business Sectors

According to a recent BBC report, cybercrime is Africa’s ‘next biggest threat’ to growth and prosperity. The report concluded that the importance of regular cyber security and data protection trainings for employees of ALL organisations in Africa has never been greater!

Even with millions spent on security infrastructure, all it takes is one employee clicking on one wrong link to compromise critical information systems. Incidents of employees inadvertently opening the ‘doors’ for hackers are on the increase!

If Hackers want to hack into your systems, they will target your employees. That is why our Cyber Security training for Non-Technical staff enables organisations to improve their security posture by equipping their employees with the necessary knowledge on how to recognize and handle the current cyber threat landscape, as well as their respective responsibilities in protecting their organisations data and information assets through proactive, security-conscious behaviour.

The Cyber Security training for Non-Technical staff are specifically formulated for the different non-technical professionals within an organisation.

  • HR Professionals
  • Hotels & Hospitality
  • Healthcare
  • Finance Professionals
  • Executives & Managers

Cyber Security for HR Professionals

According to the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD), “HR professionals handle sensitive personal data and manage other employees, so it’s crucial they are able to lead and protect their organisations from the threats of cyber attacks.”

Cybersecurity is NOT just an IT concern, as such all Employees including HR need to be fully engaged and trained to help organisations in Africa effectively mitigate risk of cyber attacks.Therefore, Dezant Consulting has created this special training course specifically to equip the HR Professionals on how to understand Cybersecurity and how to protect sensitive business data. The training will focus on how Cyber security impacts HR functions.

Training Overview


  • How to protect sensitive HR data
  • Data protection & access control
  • Cybersecurity Resource Hiring
  • Employees security awareness
  • Regulatory Compliance
  • Hiring Practices & vetting new employees
  • Dangers of Insider Threats & Disgruntled employees

Duration


1 Day (onsite or offsite) – This training could be condensed into half day if required.

Benefits


  • Creates a secure culture within your organization
  • Reduce risk & enhance system security
  • Change employee behavior / appreciation of security issues
  • Increase information security awareness & posture

Who Should Attend?


ALL Managers and Staff from the HR department of all organisation, irrespective of their position or roles within the organisation.

Cyber Security for Hotel & Hospitality Employees

The hotel industry is the next big target for cyber criminals, experts have warned, after Hilton became the fourth major hotel group to have customers’ credit card details hacked. With possible hacks into hotels point-of-sale systems, and potential access to customer personal details (such as addresses and credit cards), the hospitality and restaurant industries provide a fertile ground for potential hackers. In fact, nearly half of cyber attacks worldwide in 2017 were against small businesses with fewer than 25 workers.

As such, experts now believe businesses need to change the way they view security and make training their employees a priority. This is why Dezant Consulting has created this special one-day workshop specifically to train All your employees on how to understand Cybersecurity and how to protect sensitive business and customer data.This one day Training will focus specifically on how Cyber security impacts the Hotel & Hospitality sector.

Cyber Security for Healthcare Sector

Cyber Attacks on Healthcare is on the rise! In 2015, the Healthcare industry was the most attacked by cyber criminals according to IBM. A recent Washington Post story says healthcare organizations are juicy targets for hackers because they have a vast amount of personal information that can be used for fraud and also used to blackmail their high profile & VIP customers. This data is sold by hackers on a black market in the dark web.

In addition, Ransomware is plaguing hospitals and the health sectors, for example the Locky virus proved that a word doc can shut down computers and medical equipment at major hospitals — and demand proportionately higher ransom payments.

The number of cyber-attacks in healthcare is on the rise, and the industry must do more to prevent and respond to these incidents. This is why Dezant Consulting has created this special one-day workshop specifically to train employees in Healthcare on how to understand Cybersecurity and how to protect sensitive business data and patients private medical records. Cyber Security for Auditors, Accountants & Finance Professionals

Financial institutions and Finance Professionals such as Auditors, Accountants and Managers are particularly attractive to cyber criminals. According to PwC, financial institutions are over 30% more likely to be targeted than other companies. It’s because they deal with high-value commercial data and sensitive financial information on a daily basis,’ explains Frank Morey, chief executive of security consultancy Virtus Risk Management.

As such, Finance Professionals, both in practice and in industry, must therefore understand how to identify and respond to cyber security risks, rather than ignore the issue in the belief that cyber security is the domain of their IT departments or that their organisation’s software will prevent any breaches.This is the reason why Dezant Consulting has created this training course aimed specifically at financial industry practitioners.

  • Regulatory Compliance such as PCI & GDPR
  • Mitigating Identity Theft
  • Dangers of Insider Threats & Disgruntled employees

Duration

Benefits

Cyber Security for Executives & Managers

This course is intended for managers and leaders of organisations and businesses of any description and size. It will help Management to identify the main cyber security risks, explain why they are relevant and offer guidance on how to manage them. The course will include active discussions on recent Cyber Security topics& issues.


Training Overview


Delegates will learn how to:

  • Be aware of the threats faced by organizations and individuals
  • Understand what Cyber Security is and the terminology used around it
  • Understand some of the ways that these threats can affect their businesses
  • Learn ways by which an organisation can protect itself in a cost-effective way
  • Understand the legal, regulatory and management responsibilities for protecting the business and therefore protecting the country.

Duration


1 Day (onsite or offsite) – This training could be condensed into half day if required.


Benefits


This course prepares members of the board, senior executives, and management to understand, assess, and take a proactive posture in cyber security. With this training, you gain the fundamental knowledge and skills to investigate risk assessment and management frameworks that help mitigate dangers, as well as identify potential security gaps that could prove a liability.


Who Should Attend?


It is suitable for all levels of management (including C-level), senior leaders, information managers, risk managers and anyone in an organisation responsible for company or client data. Participants will understand more about the risks of doing business in the modern interconnected & digital world.
