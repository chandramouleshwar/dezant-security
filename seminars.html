Why Business Continuity Seminar?

Every organization needs a business continuity plan to protect its critical operations, resources and services in the event of unexpected disasters and business disruptions. At the time of a disaster everyone in the organization relies on the business continuity plan to ensure continuity of business and to restore normal operating conditions. It is critically essential to build a plan that is reliable, comprehensive, and effective for any business disruption scenario.


Our Training Seminar will be delivered in conjunction with the world famous Business Resilience Certification Consortium International (BRCCI), Canada; and it will provide you with the practical insight to build a mature, comprehensive, and reliable Business Continuity Plan with significant value added materials to assist you long after the course is over.


Our package consists of two complimentary modules:

Module 1: Business Continuity Planning and Management (CBRM) – offers step-by-step training on how to develop comprehensive, effective and reliable business continuity plans and to establish a business continuity program designed to protect organizations from unexpected business disruptions and disasters.


Module 2: Business Continuity Audit Seminar (CBRA) – offers a comprehensive overview of the Business Continuity Audit.


Seminar Objectives:

Participants will learn how to:

  • Develop comprehensive, effective and reliable business continuity plans and to establish a business continuity program designed to protect organizations from unexpected business disruptions.
  • Ensure these plans are consistent with the industry standards and best practices.
  • Comply with internal and external business continuity audit requirements.
  • To provide an introduction to business continuity planning and program management.

Overview

This seminar covers how to develop and manage business continuity and disaster recovery plans which comply with the industry’s best practices and standards such as:


  • ISO 22301:2012 Business Continuity Management Systems – Requirements
  • NFPA 1600: Standard on Disaster/Emergency Management and Business Continuity Programs
  • ITIL v3: Information Technology Infrastructure Library

Participants will undertake a walk-through of BC planning road-map with detailed steps for implementing a business continuity plan and program for their respective organizations. The BC planning road-map incorporates the industry’s best practices and guidelines for the BCM and the six stages of the BCP process as detailed below:


  • Risk Management
  • Business Impact Analysis
  • Business Continuity Strategy Development
  • Business Continuity Plan Development
  • Business Continuity Plan Testing
  • Business Continuity Plan Maintenance

Learning Objectives

Upon completion, participants will be able to:


  • Establish BC program lifecycle processes within your organization.
  • Assess business and technology requirements for a BC plan.
  • Evaluate business continuity risks to your organization.
  • Identify and select cost-effective BC recovery strategies.
  • Organize an effective BC team.
  • Develop a BC plan document.
  • Coordinate BC plan with external entities.
  • Develop an effective test plan for the BC plan.
  • Organize and conduct successful BC plan tests.
  • Establish a process for maintaining the BC plan.
  • Implement a BC plan change management process.
  • Avoid typical mistakes that lead to BC program failures.
  • Understand the main differences between a Disaster Recovery Plan, Emergency Response Plan, Crisis.
  • Management Plan, and a Business Continuity Plan.
